Lasagna

Description

The lasagna is a typicial italian dish made with meat, layers of pasta, cheese and bechamel. Mostly cooked in the oven.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Meat
  • Cheese
  • Milk
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Mix the flour with the milk
  2. Cook the meat
  3. Put the layers of pasta and meat in order
  4. Add the bechamel
  5. Put into the oven and eat
