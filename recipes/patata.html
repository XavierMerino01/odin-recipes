Patata Bullida

Description

The patata bullida is a typicial easy dish made when you are feeling sick.

Ingredients

  • Patata
  • Aigua
  • Sal
  • Pebre
  • Molta gana

Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes
  2. Boil the water
  3. Put the potatoes to boil
  4. Serve them with a pinch of salt and pepper
  5. Good luck eating it
